What to do when you miss or will miss a lab
Attending a different section
During summer session, a given lab is offered on Mon/Tue or Wed/Thu, depending on which section you are in. If you missed, or know you will have to miss an upcoming lab we will attempt to place you in the other section for that lab, in the following order of priority:

Missed lab with in-term academic concession
Missed lab with advance warning (at least two full days prior)
Missed lab without reason meriting academic concession, including redirected late arrivals
Note that the opportunity to attend the other section is available only for unexpected, and unavoidable circumstances that prevent you from attending your regularly scheduled lab. This opportunity is not meant for convenience, or prioritizing other classes. All students are expected to attend their regular lab and to schedule appointments outside of their regular lab/class times when at all possible. 
To try to arrange attending the other section, please contact your instructor (Serene) and indicate your reason for needing to attend the other section, including if your reason would merit an academic concession (__[link](https://science.ubc.ca/students/concession)__). She will confirm by the next business day if we can accommodate you in the other section. If your request cannot be accommodated, please proceed to the in-term academic concessions section below. 
If you miss a lab entirely
Complete the Prelab and Catch-up Lab
Whether or not you are granted an in-term academic concession, you are expected to remain caught up with all learning activities (pre-lab and in-lab) associated with missed labs. Complete prelabs as usual. You should also use the catch-up lab resources to complete ALL lab activities that you missed.     
In-term academic concession
If you are unable to attend or reschedule your lab, and have a reason meriting an in-term academic concession (__[link](https://science.ubc.ca/students/concession)__), please submit the following:

In-term academic concession request form
We will grant you a maximum of two in-term academic concessions for missed labs, after which we will automatically deny any further requests. This is official course policy and if you feel that your situation warrants more in-term academic concessions than two, you should appeal your case with UBC Science Advising.
If you are granted an in-term academic concession for a missed lab, any lost marks from in-lab checkpoint questions or lab notebook submissions will be shifted to your quiz grade at a weight consistent with the situation when your partner's lab notebook was graded.
